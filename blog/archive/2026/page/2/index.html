    Rotating the krbtgt password without breaking Kerberos

    There's one account in every Active Directory domain whose password signs every Kerberos ticket ever issued: krbtgt. It's disabled, nobody logs in as it, and most people never touch it — which is exactly why attackers love it. Steal the krbtgt hash and you can forge Golden Tickets: valid-looking TGTs for any user, any group, with a lifetime you choose. The only cure is to change the password.
